How to Play DigitBreaker

Objective

Crack the randomly generated 4-digit PIN. The PIN contains digits 0-9 with no repeating numbers.

Attempts

You have exactly 4 attempts to guess the correct PIN. After 4 failed attempts, the game ends.

Color Feedback System

Green: Correct number, correct position
Orange: Correct number, wrong position
Red: Number not in PIN

Getting Started

Enter your first name and surname, then click "Start Game" to begin. Input your guess one digit at a time in the boxes provided.

Pro Tip: Use the color feedback to eliminate possibilities and deduce the correct PIN!
